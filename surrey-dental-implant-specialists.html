Are You a Candidate for Dental Implant Treatments?

Adults of all ages are eligible for dental implants provided they are in good physical condition and their gums are healthy. We always carry out a thorough consultation first to determine if it is safe to go ahead with the treatment.

What Should I Do After Getting A Dental Implant?

After surgery, patients should take a break and not exert themselves. Patients should refrain from vigorously brushing or rinsing after surgery.
